Certificate Management System

Welcome to the OGT11 Certificate Management System. Follow the steps below to get started with your certificate request.

Getting Started

1

Request a User Account

First, you'll need to request a user account. This account will be used to manage your certificates and access the system.

2

Validate Your Email

After submitting your request, you'll receive an email with a validation link. Click the link to verify your email address.

3

Wait for Approval

An administrator will review your request and approve your account. You'll receive an email notification when your account is ready.

4

Request Certificates

Once your account is approved, you can return here to request certificates using your validated username.
